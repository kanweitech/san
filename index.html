Welcome to SAN Africa


Smart Agropreneurs' Network Africa

SAN Africa is one of the largest Agro business MLM cooperative Network for African's which was incorporated in August, 2018 and since then successfully trained, mentored thousands of farmers. Our core values of sharing knowledge, mentorship, Funding and giving back to the society and the world at large has touched the heart of many aspiring and burgeoning agriculturist as they also believe that agriculture is a tool for socio cultural development in Africa. We welcome you to SAN Africa and we believe we can truly explore the agro world together.

SAN Africa is an initiative targeted towards raising the next generation of successful farmers.



MISSION

To make agriculture a socio-cultural development in africa.


VISION

To raise the next generation of agropreneur that would provide solutions to the the rate of unemployment in africa and the world at large.


Training

We work with you to give an intensive classroom and practical training that allows you to be among the leading successful agropreneurs. We create a bespoke consultancy package on sustainability and livestock management.

Exposure

The agricultural enterprise is dynamic and engaging. Our goals are to ensure you have unmatched access to the industry and experts throughout africa.

Mentorship

We support, coach and advice you with one on one relationship in achieving a functional and well structured farm.

Agro Tourism

Tourism in agriculture. We often embark on tours to farms within and outside Nigeria to increase knowledge and build technological and scientific skills for future milestones.
